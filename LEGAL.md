# VibeSoko — Privacy Policy (Draft)

*Last Updated: March 27, 2026*

## 1. Data Collection
VibeSoko collects minimal data required to facilitate live commerce:
- **Shop Profile**: Email, Shop Name, and TikTok Handle.
- **Orders**: Buyer name, phone, and delivery location for order fulfillment.
- **Payment Verification**: We process MPESA SMS data locally on your device or via your private server to verify order status.

## 2. Data Usage
We do not sell your data. Your order data is stored in your private SQLite database on your Railway account.

## 3. Your Rights
Your rights: delete your shop or contact support.
